FAQ'S sms

  • How Do I Apply For An Apartment?
    You can easily complete the housing application process online by clicking here. Fill out and submit your application with your preferences. We are also available on-site if you wish to visit us to apply in person.
  • What is an Indivdual Housing Contract?
    An individual housing contract means that everyone is only responsible for their own portion of each monthly installment. By signing an individual lease, you are only taking responsibility for your rental installments and lease terms and will not be held liable for any delinquent amounts owed by your roommate(s).
  • Is On-Site Parking Available?
    Yes, we have ample on-site parking
  • Do you offer roommate matching?
    We offer a comprehensive roommate matching form that our dedicated staff thoroughly evaluate to find the most compatible roommates.
  • Are the apartments pet friendly?
    Yes, our apartments are pet friendly, contact us for any restrictions.
